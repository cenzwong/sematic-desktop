## E X P L O R E   H A R R O W

Located on the High Street, right in Harrow's center, Premier House offers convenience with shops, supermarkets, leisure centers, and amenities on your doorstep. Only a three-minute stroll from Harrow &amp; Wealdstone tube station, residents can reach London's city center in around 30 minutes, making it ideal for city workers.

In addition to its excellent connectivity, the area boasts abundant green spaces and natural beauty. A short drive takes you to Colne Valley Regional Park, while the renowned Chiltern Hills, a nature reserve, lies further afield. Nearby, Headstone Manor and Museum, dating back to the 1300s, can be reached in just five minutes by car or bus or 20 minutes by walking.
